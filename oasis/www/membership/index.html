Membership

Joining OASIS means becoming part of a vibrant network committed to leveraging Free and Open-Source Software (FOSS) for social impact. Whether you’re an individual, organization, or institution, membership offers an opportunity to contribute, collaborate, and grow.

Who Can Join?

FOSS Technologists & Innovators

Individuals and organizations developing or implementing FOSS solutions tailored for the social sector.

Technology Providers & Vendors

Companies offering FOSS-based services, tools, and consultation.

Philanthropic Institutions

Organizations looking to build tech capacity among their beneficiaries.

Social Sector Organizations

NGOs, nonprofits, and other citizen sector entities eager to adopt FOSS for transformative impact.
