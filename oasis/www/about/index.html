About OASIS

By adopting FOSS, organisations in the social sector can leverage powerful tools, reduce costs, and contribute to a collaborative ecosystem of technological innovation.

Mission

To address systemic technological gaps and lack of technological capacity in the social sector through the adoption of FOSS.

To help organizations attain technological self-sufficiency to better focus on their social work and increase its impact

To help create a thriving ecosystem of FOSS innovation and collaboration in the social sector, made of diverse stakeholders.

Organisation

OASIS is a growing alliance of a diverse set of organizations led by a governing council. 

Board

The strategic direction of the alliance, its day to day activities and plans are directed by the board. The current board (formed in 2024) was constituted by the founding member organisations of OASIS. The framework for the constitution or reconstitution of the council, such as an election process, is still underway.
